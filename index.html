Hot tub

The hot tub is free to use.

It is always nice to sit there an enjoy the view.

It is simple to handle. Just remove the cover, and jump in. The temperature, jets and light can be adjusted from the panel.

Two simple rules for using the hot tube:

Shower before and after use.

No glasses, food or other thinks that can cause damage is allowed in the hot tube.

After use, be sure that the cover is put back and secured with the strap every time. The wind can be very strong and will destroy the cover if it's not secured with the strap.

Trouble shooting

How do I check in?

Check-in is a breeze! You'll receive a code to the key box beside the entry door 24-48 hours prior to arrival for seamless entry.

What about check out?

Simply leave the cabin by 11 AM. Put the key back in the key box. Check the check out check list.

No water in the kitchen tap?

To prevent water damage there is a water guard system in the kitchen. If you spill water on the floor near the dishwasher it will probably cut off the water supply. The same thing will happen if you spill water inside the cabinet under the sink.

There are two sensors, one in the cabinet and one under the dishwasher.

To reset this system, wipe off every drop of water from the sensor with some dry paper and push the grey buttons on the black disks in the cabinet under the sink.

To get to the sensor under the dishwasher you must remove the base off the kitchen furniture. The base is two pieces, and the split is in the middle. Gently drag the two pieces from the split towards you. You will then see the sensor under the dishwasher.

The hob/ coocktop is not working?

There is a sensor in the ceiling above the cooktop - it switches off the power to the hob/ cooktop in the event of overheating and with a power outage. The switch to RESET it, is on the side of the box. Use something long to push the button.
